Das Lui

Welcome to Das Lui, your cozy corner in the Luisental student residence in Bremen! 🌟 Whether you're up for a game of kicker (foosball), darts, or just want to hang out with friends over some cold drinks, we've got you covered. 🍻

Run by students for everyone, we’re open every Wednesday and Friday from 8 PM. ⏰ Our doors are open to all—come as you are, and enjoy a friendly and inclusive space where cultures meet and new friendships are made. 🌍🤝
